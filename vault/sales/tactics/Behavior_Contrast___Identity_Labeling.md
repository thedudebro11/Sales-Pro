# Behavior Contrast / Identity Labeling

## Definition
Framing two identities — 'broke energy' vs 'booked energy' — forces the viewer to self-identify and creates aspirational tension. People don't want to see themselves as the passive, weak version, so they lean into the confident approach being modeled.
