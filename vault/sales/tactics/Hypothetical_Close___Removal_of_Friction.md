# Hypothetical Close / Removal of Friction

## Definition
Asking 'if money or time weren't a factor, would you already be saying yes?' bypasses logical objections and surfaces emotional desire. If they say yes, the only barrier is practical — and practical barriers can be solved together.

## When to Use


## Example Quote
> "Can I ask that if money or time weren't a factor, would you already be saying yes?"
