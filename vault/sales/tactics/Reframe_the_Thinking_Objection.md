# Reframe the Thinking Objection

## Definition
Repositioning 'let me think about it' from a price concern to a 'will this work for me' concern disarms the prospect and shifts the conversation to proof and fit rather than negotiation, making it easier to close in the moment.

## When to Use


## Example Quote
> "Most people who think about it aren't thinking about price. They're thinking, will this work for me? So let's answer that right now."
