# Article and PDF Source Strategy

Videos are useful for delivery and persuasion patterns.

Articles and PDFs are useful for facts, rules, data, compliance, and current SEO reality.

## Good sources to add

- Google Business Profile official help pages
- Google Search Central documentation
- FTC advertising and email compliance guidance
- Cold email compliance explainers from reputable legal/business sources
- Local SEO industry studies with dates
- Website speed/conversion research with source and date
- Case studies with actual numbers and screenshots
- Your own audit reports and call notes

## Bad sources to treat carefully

- “Guaranteed SEO ranking” agency blogs
- Sales influencers making broad claims
- Old SEO articles
- Unsourced statistics
- Viral Twitter/LinkedIn claims
- Generic marketing listicles

## How to ingest written sources

Run articles/PDF text through:

1. `09_ARTICLE_PDF_INGEST.md`
2. `02_EXTRACT_CLAIMS.md`
3. `03_GRADE_CLAIMS.md`

Do not let an article become a sales claim until it passes claim grading.
