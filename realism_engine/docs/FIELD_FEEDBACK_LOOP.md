# Field Feedback Loop

The strongest data in Sales Pro will come from your own calls.

After every outreach session, record:

- Who you called
- Industry
- Hook used
- What they said in the first 10 seconds
- Objection heard
- Whether they stayed on the phone
- Whether they asked for info
- Whether they booked
- Exact phrases that worked
- Exact phrases that failed

## Weekly review

Once per week, run the field notes through `08_REAL_CALL_FEEDBACK.md`.

Look for patterns:

- Which hooks get owners to stay on the phone?
- Which lines trigger “send me info”?
- Which industries respond better to soft vs direct language?
- Which objections repeat?
- Which CTA feels easiest to say?

## Rule

Real call results beat sales influencer advice.

If a guru says one thing but Tucson business owners react differently, trust the field.
