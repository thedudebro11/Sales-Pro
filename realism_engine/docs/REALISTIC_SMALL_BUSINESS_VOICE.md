# Realistic Small Business Voice Guide

## The owner’s likely mindset

A small business owner is usually:

- Busy
- Skeptical
- Interrupted constantly
- Tired of marketing pitches
- Burned by previous SEO/web people
- Protective of their time and money
- Interested only if the message is specific and practical

## What sounds believable

- “I looked at your site before calling.”
- “I noticed a couple things that may be worth fixing.”
- “I can show you quickly.”
- “You can decide if it matters.”
- “No pressure — you’ll at least know what I’d fix first.”

## What sounds fake

- “I’m not trying to sell you anything.”
- “I know exactly why you’re losing customers.”
- “You’re handing business to your competitors.”
- “I can fix your Google ranking.”
- “I only have two spots left.”

## Best cold call opener pattern

```text
Hey [Name], this is Michael with Precision Workz. I pulled up [Business Name] before calling and noticed a couple things on your website and Google profile that may be making people less likely to call. Got 30 seconds?
```

## Best audit CTA pattern

```text
Would it be crazy to spend 15 minutes this week looking at it together? I’ll show you what I found, what I’d fix first, and you can decide if it matters.
```

## Tone target

Not timid. Not hypey.

Calm, specific, and useful.
