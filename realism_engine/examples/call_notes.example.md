# Field Feedback Example

Date: 2026-06-27
Business type: Local contractor
Script used: Website + GBP audit cold call

## Call result

Owner answered but sounded busy.

## What I said

“Hey John, this is Michael with Precision Workz. I pulled up your business online before calling and noticed a couple things on your website and Google profile that may be making people less likely to call. Got 30 seconds?”

## Prospect reaction

He said: “Make it quick.”

## What worked

- “I pulled up your business online” got attention.
- “Got 30 seconds?” was better than asking for 15 minutes immediately.

## What failed

- When I mentioned Google ranking too early, he got skeptical.
- He asked me to send info instead of booking.

## Objection heard

“Just send me something.”

## Follow-up idea

Send a short text with one specific observation and offer to show the rest in 15 minutes.
