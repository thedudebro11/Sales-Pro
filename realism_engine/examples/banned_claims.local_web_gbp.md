# Banned Claim Bank — Local Web + GBP

Do not use these unless the user provides verified proof or a source.

## Ranking guarantees

- I can get you top 3 on Google Maps.
- We guarantee top 3 Map Pack ranking.
- Your business will show up above competitors.
- Google will start showing you to ready-to-buy customers.
- You will dominate Google Maps.

## Unsupported statistics

- The top 3 get 70% of all clicks.
- The top 3 get 80% of local clicks.
- Position 4 gets almost zero traffic.
- Every second of load time costs X% of customers.
- You are losing X dollars per month.

## Fake proof

- I helped local businesses go from invisible to top 3.
- I took a business from 2 leads a week to 11.
- My clients always get more calls.
- Businesses I work with rank faster than their competitors.

## Overaggressive language

- You are handing customers to competitors every day.
- Your website is actively killing your business.
- Your Google profile is broken.
- Google is punishing you.
- I know exactly why you are not ranking.
- I can fix it.

## Fake scarcity

- I only have two spots open this week, unless that is actually true.
- This opportunity will disappear.
- Your competitors are closing the gap every day, unless backed by real market observation.

## Safer replacements

Risky: “I can get you top 3.”
Safe: “I can help improve the controllable factors that make you more competitive in local search.”

Risky: “You are losing customers every day.”
Safe: “There are a few issues that may be making people less likely to call.”

Risky: “Google is showing competitors because your profile is incomplete.”
Safe: “An incomplete profile can make it harder for Google and customers to understand what you offer.”
