# Approved Claim Bank — Local Web + GBP

These claims are safe for early-stage outreach.

## Safe claims

- I noticed a few fixable issues with your website and Google profile.
- I can show you what may be making customers less likely to call.
- I can compare your website and Google profile against the businesses showing above you.
- I can give you a prioritized list of the top 3 to 5 fixes I would make first.
- I can help improve the controllable factors that influence local visibility.
- I can make your website clearer, faster, more mobile-friendly, and easier to contact from.
- I can help make your Google Business Profile more complete and useful for customers.
- I can help your homepage explain what you do, where you serve, and how to contact you faster.
- I can show you the difference between looking decent and actually converting visitors into calls.
- I can help make your online presence more competitive.

## Safe Google wording

- “more competitive in local search”
- “improve the controllable factors”
- “may be holding you back”
- “visibility factors”
- “profile completeness”
- “competitor comparison”
- “local presence”
- “searchers comparing you against competitors”

## Safe CTA language

- “Worth taking a quick look this week?”
- “Would it be crazy to spend 15 minutes looking at it?”
- “Want me to send over a quick audit slot?”
- “I can show you what I found and you can decide if it matters.”
- “No pressure — you will at least walk away knowing what I would fix first.”
