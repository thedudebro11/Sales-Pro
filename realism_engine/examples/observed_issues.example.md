# Observed Issues Example

Business: Example Roofing Tucson
Industry: Roofing
City: Tucson, AZ

## Website observations

- Mobile homepage loads slowly on first visit.
- Phone number is present but not sticky on mobile.
- Main CTA is below the fold.
- No clear service area statement near the top of the homepage.
- No obvious before/after project photos on the homepage.
- Website looks visually older than two competitors ranking nearby.

## Google Business Profile observations

- Service list appears incomplete.
- Photos look outdated or sparse.
- Business has reviews but owner responses are inconsistent.
- Competitor profiles have more service detail and more recent photos.

## Safe outreach angle

I noticed a few things on your website and Google profile that may be making it harder for people to choose you when they compare roofers online.

## Do not say

- Your website is costing you customers every week.
- I know exactly why your competitor is beating you.
- I can get you top 3.
- Google is punishing your profile.
