# Manual Test — Small Business Realism

Read the opener out loud.

It fails if:

- It takes more than 35 seconds before asking a question.
- It sounds like a webinar pitch.
- It uses exact stats you cannot prove.
- It claims the owner is losing customers without evidence.
- It promises rankings.
- It sounds too polished to say naturally.

It passes if the owner could reasonably respond:

> “Okay, what did you notice?”
